Misal Pav

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sprouted moth beans
  • 2 cups mixed sprouts (matki, moth, and black-eyed peas)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/4 cup farsan (sev)
  • 2-3 slices of bread, toasted

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Add sprouts, misal masala, and salt. Cook until the sprouts are tender.
  5. Serve hot with toasted bread, chopped onions, cilantro, and farsan.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 4 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers), boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  4. Add mashed potatoes, mashed vegetables, pav bhaji masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, adding water as needed. Mash everything together.
  6. Serve hot with buttered pav (buns) and chopped onions.
